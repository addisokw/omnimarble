freerouting's earlier "unusable" verdict was wrong — its failures were fixable pipeline defects (it was fed the plane nets, had no pass cap, and ripped up our critical copper). Fix: a prepared DSN (real net classes, 1.0 mm pulse clearance, critical copper locked type fix), max_passes=30 (the 9999 default was the 700 GB / non-termination cause), on JDK 26. Same DSN also uploads to DeepPCB unchanged.
