Known compromises

  • 01
    Silkscreen overlaps deferred. Real cause is footprint body outlines, not text — bulk API edit made DRC worse (added text-height violations) and was reverted. Belongs in fab-prep.
  • 02
    118 nets unrouted, not shorted. Deliberate trade: the router converts every would-be short into a visible airwire. Honest missing copper beats a hidden defect.
  • 03
    Autorouted, not hand-tuned. Track geometry is machine-optimal for connectivity, not aesthetics or controlled-impedance. Fine for this SELV pulse board.
  • 04
    Order-time part still open. 6.8k bleed C-number + relay NO/NC continuity remain review gates before assembly.
